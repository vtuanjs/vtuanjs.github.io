This is the first post in my **Product space**. Here I'll write about product
thinking, discovery, prioritisation, and the messy reality of shipping.

## How to add a new product post

1. Create a Markdown file in `src/content/product/`, e.g. `my-new-post.md`.
2. Add front matter:

   ```yaml
   ---
   title: "My new post"
   description: "A one-line summary used as the SEO meta description."
   date: 2026-07-01
   ---
   ```
3. Write your content in Markdown below.

It shows up automatically on the [Product space](/product/) page and the home page.
